The hub is a community-centric business, owned by a Farmer Producer Organization (FPO) and operated on an accessible pay-per-use model.

  • ‚úì
    Pay-Per-Use Access: Farmers pay a small fee per crate for storage or per kg for drying, de-risking technology adoption.
  • ‚úì
    Market Linkage: The hub acts as an aggregation point, enabling direct sales to high-value buyers and increasing farmer bargaining power.
  • ‚úì
    Quality Improvement: On-site facilities for sorting, grading, and improved packaging to meet formal market standards.
  • ‚úì
    From Price Takers to Market Makers: The hub transforms farmers from isolated sellers into a collective market force.
